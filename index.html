About Phone

Infinix Mobile is a smartphone company with headquarters based in China, founded in 2013 by Transsion Holdings.[1][2][3] Their phones are manufactured in several countries including France, India, Indonesia, South Korea, China, Pakistan and Egypt. They have research and development centers in France and Korea and design their phones in France. Infinix Mobile is available in Asia and approximately 30 countries in the Middle East, Africa and also in the United States.[4][5][6] The company was also the first smartphone brand to manufacture in Pakistan
